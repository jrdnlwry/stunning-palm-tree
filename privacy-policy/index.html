Privacy Policy

How CrawlWise handles homeowner information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information CrawlWise may collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you read our guides, request crawl space help, or contact us.

Policy sections

Overview

CrawlWise is an educational crawl space content platform. Most visitors can read our guides without submitting personal information. If you choose to use a form, request help, or contact us, we may collect information needed to respond to your request.

This policy is written for the current demo site and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before launch, especially if the site will collect leads, use advertising pixels, or operate in jurisdictions with specific privacy requirements.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, ZIP code, crawl space concerns, uploaded photos, or messages submitted through a contact or help request form.

We may also collect basic technical information automatically, such as browser type, device information, pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, referring page, and usage patterns that help us understand how visitors use the site.

How we use information

We may use information to provide educational content, respond to inquiries, route help requests, improve website usability, measure content performance, prevent spam or misuse, and communicate about services or resources related to your request.

If you submit a request for crawl space help, we may use the details you provide to understand the issue and determine whether a relevant professional or service provider may be appropriate.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information as a standalone product. If you ask to be connected with crawl space help, we may share relevant request details with service providers, lead-routing partners, or operational vendors who help respond to your inquiry.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to enforce site terms, or as part of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

Cookies and analytics

CrawlWise may use cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies to remember preferences, understand traffic sources, improve content, and measure how pages perform. Browser settings may allow you to block or delete cookies, though some features may not work as intended.

If advertising, remarketing, or third-party tracking tools are added later, this policy should be updated to describe those tools and any opt-out options.

Your choices

You can choose not to submit forms or provide optional information. You may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your inquiry, subject to verification and applicable legal requirements.

If you receive communications from us, follow the unsubscribe instructions where provided or contact us directly to ask that we stop non-transactional messages.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at contact@example.com. Please include enough detail for us to identify and respond to your request.
