About

A practical homeowner guide to what’s happening under the house.

CrawlWise helps homeowners understand crawl space problems before they hire someone, approve a repair scope, or ignore a warning sign. The site is built around plain-English education, cost context, DIY checks, professional warning signs, and contractor questions.

We do not present ourselves as a single local contractor. In some cases, we may connect homeowners with crawl space professionals in their area. That handoff should be transparent, optional, and secondary to helping readers understand the issue.

What we cover

Crawl space repair, waterproofing, mold remediation, vapor barriers, encapsulation, drainage, floor sagging, joist concerns, and moisture control.

What this site does not replace

This website is not a substitute for a licensed inspection, structural engineer, mold professional, electrician, HVAC contractor, pest professional, or crawl space contractor.
